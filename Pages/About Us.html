Pitmaster Jeremy Staedler


After more than 20 years in the restaurant industry, it was time to move on. Time to move forward. Time to light a fire. Jeremy is excited to present his passion for craft barbecue to the world. Following the typical "quit his job to pursue his dream" storyline, he produces not-so-typical barbecue. Combining a disciplined attention to detail with traditional Central Texas barbecue techniques, he consistently creates outstanding barbecue that is not often found 'round these parts.



Smoke and Meat BBQ

Smoke & Meat


At Smoke & Meat BBQ, we strive to bring you the best Central-Texas BBQ we can produce. We think it's pretty good. We use simple, traditional live-fire methods... we're just very particular about them. From our simple, traditional S&P Brisket, to our meticulously crafted Smoked Links, no corners are cut (well, we do trim the sharp corners off the brisket, so I guess there's that?), no fillers or injections needed, no automation required, just straight-forward, Central-Texas style BBQ. Just smoke from burning wood & meat. And some paprika sometimes.

We took inspiration from the best of the best Cen-Tex BBQ joints and brought them home to Cali. From Franklin's smoked brisket, to L&L's beef cheeks (these little bundles of meaty heaven will change the way you think about BBQ. For reals. It's kiiiinda crazy.), we've got what you may not know you need for catering your next event, or if you come by for one of our pop-ups. All of our meats are either smoked on our offset smoker or a Santa Maria grill (we ARE still in California, after all) using locally sourced Oak, Almond, and Cherry. We smoke the good stuff out here too, Tootsie.
